Angela's Recipe for the Best Cinnamon Rolls


Ingredients


For the dough:

  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 2 1/4 yeast
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the filling:

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup butter

Instruction

  1. Mix the milk with yeast eggs and sugar.
  2. Melt the butter and add to the mixture.
  3. Add in the flour
  4. knead the dough for 10 minutes.
  5. transfer the dougn into bowl.
  6. serve the recipe.
